We help clients analyze and identify fundamental issues and key areas where development projects should be undertaken and advise, formulate, implement, monitor the development projects and also carry out Post-Project reviews and evaluations to measure the impact of such Projects.

Impact is the Key

Success of any Development Project will be measured on its Impact with regard to pre-identified objectives. It is a common issue among many Projects where the community is involved, particularly the community development projects that the impact with regard to its specific objectives is hard to achieve. We help in formulating, implementing, development of procedures and systems, monitoring, assessing and auditing etc. so that the objectives of the Project are achieved effectively and efficiently.

Sustainability through Participation

In the arena of Development Projects aimed at SED, a unique challenge has been the sustainability. Economic and social sustainability is essential to ensure the long-term impact of Project which could only be possible with effective community participation. Our strategic thinking workshops and brainstorming sessions have enormously helped the clients to discover avenues to ensure community participation and economic and social sustainability.

Project implementing is a challenge. The territory, political, social and economic environment, resource requirements and sources of funding are factors that influence the success of Project implementation. We excel in blending and maneuvering these factors to make the Project a success.

Recognizing the Optimum Solution

We believe that clients need more than they express. Therefore we use a unique method to recognize the optimum solution by viewing the client’s need in 3 different angles. Client’s point of view, the first approach, basically gives us rough idea and guidelines for recognition of the optimum solution. Secondly we look at the problem in consultant’s point of view, which is very broad and analytical. In this stage skeleton of the optimum solution is defined. Finally, we view the problem in the web master’s point of view. Here we check the technical feasibility of the solution and other practical issues. All three views finally lead us to recognize the optimum solution.

What is a Strategy?

A strategy enables a company or an individual to ensure that day to day decisions fit in with the long-term interest of the organisation or the individual. Strategic decisions have positive impact on future results. Therefore strategic planning is key for the future development and progress and it takes place at the highest management level of the organisation.

Are you a Strategist?

Strategists look at what is happening now in the context of where they want to go. A good Strategist is a team player, skilled communicator, keeps calm when unexpected occurs, balances short and long-term needs and sees problems as opportunities to improve. MF natures and develops business strategists.
