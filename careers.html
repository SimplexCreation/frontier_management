OUR HR POLICY

Core skills

Our core activity is solving complex business problems.This requires people with strong analytical skills and innovative synthesizing skills.The entry requirement for a consulting career at Management Frontiers is strong analytical skills. Demand for synthesizing skills will grow as one progresses up in the career.

Career path

There are three groups of personnel at Management Frontiers: Analysts, Consultants and Advisors, Associates and Directors.

Transparent remuneration

Consulting staff remuneration is directly linked to their billing rates. Increasing cost recovery achievements lead to automatic remuneration enhancements.

Revenue sharing

Both consulting and support staff at Management Frontiers enjoys every month a direct share of realized revenue.
